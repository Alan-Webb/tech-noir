Let me introduce myself

I discovered my passion for coding in the unexpected environment of a genetics lab. Working with the coding language of DNA inspired me to learn other coding languages and become the web developer I am today. Since retiring from bioscience research I have developed my front-end skills set and I am continuing to learn and refine skills everyday. Looking forward, I am excited about the future of web development and all the wonderful learning opportunities it may bring. Everyday I build and revise projects and skills, both new and old, so that I may make meaningful contributions to the web dev industry.
